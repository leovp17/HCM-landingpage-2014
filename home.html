What problem does HCMFront resolve?

Human Resource departments generally lack appropriate tools to adequately measure, structure and manage human capital. This generates:

  • Clumsy and out-of-date data bases,
  • Late decision making with unreliable information,
  • Difficulties tracing processes from beginning to end, and,
  • Little visibility toward the rest of the organization, and large, long-term skill losses.

HCMFront seeks to reduce costs by digitizing processes and providing the tools to maintain robust, up-to-date and systematic data bases. This will help increase learning about each process and will help companies objectively improve their systems.

What is the difference between HCMFront and other Human Resource softwares?

HCMFront is a software that fosters a collaborative and friendly work environment. It does not require installations, specific hardware acquisitions, license payments or updating costs.

It works on mobile devices, across various browsers and operating systems in order to facilitate and improve human capital management.

How do I use HCMFront?

Thanks to its configuration, HCMFront lets you to create multiple users within an organization with predetermined roles and permissions. It is capable of consolidating a massive portion of Human Resource areas in one single place, also allowing for the integration of important stakeholders like clients and external agencies.

Who owns my information in HCMFront?

You do! The information input in HCMFront pertains 100% to the organization, forever. We maintain an extremely strict privacy policy that is explained in detail with each client.
